CrystalsCollector


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four cryastals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of point to you total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystalk is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each of the crystals.
